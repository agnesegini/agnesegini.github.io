Welcome to my webpage!

My name is Agnese Gini and I am a postdoctoral researcher at the Interdisciplinary Centre for Security, Reliability and Trust of University of Luxembourg, under the supervision of Prof. Jean-Sébastien Coron.
I am part of the Applied Crypto Group

My main research interests are

  • lattice based cryptography,
  • computer algebra for applications in classic and post-quantum cryptography,
  • computational number theory.
